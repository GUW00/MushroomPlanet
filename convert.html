? How it works

This is an on-chain swap, not an AMM. The contract holds a reserve of both tokens and swaps at a fixed 1:100 ratio permanently written into the contract code. Two wallet confirmations are required: one to approve the token spend and one to execute the swap.

vs Off-chain convert

The off-chain !convert bot command swaps in-game balances inside your Sporebot wallet. This page swaps real on-chain tokens in your Polygon wallet — useful if you hold tokens outside the bot or prefer a trustless option.

! Reserve limits

Swaps will revert cleanly if the contract reserve is too low to cover the output. No tokens are ever lost on a failed swap. The contract can be paused instantly by the treasury if needed.
