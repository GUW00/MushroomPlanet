How It Works
Three Stages of Governance
💬
Stage 1 — Pre-Proposal
Free · Discord only · No voting

Post your idea in #pre-proposals on Discord. Gauge community interest organically. If it gets traction, submit an Official Proposal.
📋
Stage 2 — Official Proposal
6874 SPORE burn · 5 days · 1 person = 1 vote

Formalize your idea here. Requires 42 users to vote and 70% must say yes to advance. Costs SPORE to prevent spam.
🗳️
Stage 3 — Official Vote
Free · 5 days · MVP weighted

The binding governance vote. Every voter's MVP score is their vote weight. You must have MVP > 0 to participate. Earn SPORE rewards for voting.
⚡
Mycelium Voting Power
Score: 0 – 100 points

Your MVP is calculated across 7 categories. Use !mvp in Discord to see your current score and breakdown.

In Official Votes, your MVP score is your vote weight. A voter with 80 MVP carries 80x the weight of someone with 1 MVP.
📊
How MVP is Calculated
Mycelium Nodes — 25 pts max (0.5 per node)
Staked SPORE — 20 pts max (0.1 per 10M)
Planted SHROOM — 15 pts max (0.1 per 100K)
Onchain SPORE — 10 pts max (0.1 per 10M)
Farm Rank — 10 pts max
Pixie XP — 10 pts max (0.1 per 100K XP)
Onchain SHROOM — 5 pts max
NFTs — 5 pts max
🏆
Why MVP Matters
The winning option is whichever side accumulates the highest total MVP sum — not just the most votes.

Voters are rewarded with SPORE for participating. Higher MVP = larger reward.
