Requirements

Free Trial & Requirements

New users get a 42-day free trial to farm with no requirements. After that, meet ONE of the following to keep farming:

  • Option 1: Hold 500,000+ SHROOM-equivalent on-chain for 3+ consecutive farming days
  • Option 2: Hold 2 Sporebot NFTs or any 1 Mooshie NFT — verified for 3+ days
  • Option 3: Provide liquidity on QuickSwap (LP Bonus of 0.1 or higher) — qualifies immediately

Which option is right for you?

Option 1 is the easiest and cheapest for most new users — you just need to hold SHROOM in your wallet. You're not giving anything to Mushroom Planet, just keeping tokens you already own on-chain. Buy a small amount on QuickSwap, hold it, and you're set.

Option 2 is best if you want NFT perks alongside farming access. Sporebots are the easiest to acquire as they are still available — Mooshie NFTs (House, King, Queen) are sold out from primary and only available on the secondary market via OpenSea.

Option 3 is ideal if you're already providing liquidity — it qualifies you instantly with no waiting period and also earns you weekly LP reward airdrops on top. View the full LP guide here.

On-Chain vs Off-Chain

🟢 On-Chain

Stored directly on the Polygon blockchain. SHROOMS, SPORES, and NFTs in your Web3 wallet are fully decentralized — you own them, you can send, swap, or sell anytime. Costs small POL gas fees for transactions.

🟡 Off-Chain

Stored inside the Sporebot game database. SHROOMS and SPORES earned from !forage, !farm, or Reddit upvotes live here. No gas fees, instant rewards — managed by Sporebot until you withdraw.

Get a Wallet

Getting a Web3 Wallet

A Web3 wallet is your blockchain address — it stores your SHROOMS, SPORES, and NFTs, and lets you connect to apps like QuickSwap or OpenSea.

Option 1 — Use Your Reddit Vault

If you use Reddit's mobile app, you already have a Vault. Open the Reddit app, tap your Profile, then Vault, then the gear icon to find your Public Address and Recovery Phrase.

  • Your Public Address (0x...) is safe to share — this is what you register with Sporebot
  • Your Recovery Phrase is secret — whoever has it controls the wallet
  • Never share it, never store it in email, screenshots, or the cloud — write it down
  • You can import your Vault into MetaMask or Trust Wallet using the Recovery Phrase

Option 2 — Create a New Wallet

  • Download MetaMask (browser extension or mobile), Trust Wallet, or Coinbase Wallet
  • Tap Create a Wallet and save your 12-word Secret Recovery Phrase securely
  • Make sure your wallet is set to the Polygon Network

Once you have a wallet, use !gas in the Reddit Daily Forage Thread to receive 0.01 POL to cover your first transaction fees — one-time, free.

Provide Liquidity

Adding Tokens to LP

Providing liquidity means depositing equal values of two tokens into a pool. You earn a share of trading fees and qualify for weekly Sporebot LP reward airdrops.

  • On QuickSwap go to Pool and select V2
  • Choose a pair: $HROOM/WETH or SPR/WETH
  • Enter equal dollar values of both tokens (e.g. $100 SHROOM + $100 WETH)
  • Approve both tokens, click Supply, confirm in your wallet
  • You'll receive LP tokens representing your share of the pool

Managing Your Position

  • Add more: Repeat the supply steps above at any time
  • Remove: Go to Remove Liquidity on QuickSwap, select your tokens, confirm
  • LP tokens accumulate trading fees automatically while you hold them

Minimum for LP Rewards

  • SHROOM/WETH pool: 10,000 SHROOM minimum
  • SPORE/WETH pool: 100,000 SPORE minimum
  • Holding 0.1%+ of either pool also qualifies you for to continue using Sporebot
Impermanent Loss

Understanding Impermanent Loss

Impermanent Loss (IL) happens when the price of the tokens in your pool changes, causing the value of your LP position to be less than if you had just held the tokens in your wallet.

Example

  • You start with $20 in SHROOM and split it: $10 SHROOM + $10 WETH in the pool
  • SHROOM goes up 60% (now $16), WETH goes up 20% (now $12)
  • The pool auto-rebalances to maintain 50/50 — you end up with ~$14 SHROOM + $14 WETH = $28
  • If you had just held the $20 of SHROOM, it would now be worth $32
  • The $4 difference is your Impermanent Loss

IL is "impermanent" because if prices return to their original ratio, the loss disappears. Weekly LP reward airdrops help offset IL over time.

Weekly LP Rewards

Weekly LP Reward Airdrops

Providing LP automatically qualifies you for weekly Sporebot reward airdrops proportional to your pool share. Snapshot every Sunday — airdrop sent Monday.

  • SHROOM/WETH pool: 3.5M SHROOMS distributed weekly (Year 2)
  • SPORE/WETH pool: 500M SPORES distributed weekly (Year 1)
  • Your rewards = your % share of the pool
  • Example: 5% of the pool = 5% of the weekly rewards

Farm System

Farm System Overview

Your planted SHROOMS passively earn SPORES based on your Spore Generation Rate (SGR). The farm advances through 5 stages over 42 days. Harvest at Sporulation to rank up and increase your plant limit permanently.

Stages: Spore → Germination → Mycelium → Fruiting → Sporulation

Farm Ranks & Plant Limits

  • Bronze: 0% SGR | 2,000,000 plant limit
  • Silver: +3% SGR | 2,500,000 plant limit
  • Gold: +6% SGR | 3,000,000 plant limit
  • Emerald: +9% SGR | 3,500,000 plant limit
  • Diamond: +12% SGR | 4,200,000 plant limit
  • Master: +15% SGR | 4,500,000 plant limit
  • Grandmaster: +20% SGR | 5,000,000 plant limit
  • Legend: +25% SGR | 6,874,000 plant limit

After every harvest, replant all your SHROOMS to resume earning.

SGR Sources

Spore Generation Rate (SGR)

Your planted SHROOMS earn passive SPORES based on your SGR. Higher SGR = more passive SPORES from the same planted SHROOMS. The in-game cap is 100% SGR.

In-Game SGR Sources

  • Farm Rank: up to 20%
  • Farm Stage: up to 20% (Sporulation)
  • Potion Brew: up to 10%
  • 5 Gemstones: up to 25% (5% each)
  • Sporebot Upgrades: up to 25% (5% per category)

On-Chain SGR Bonuses

  • LP (SHROOM/WETH or SPORE/WETH): proportional bonus
  • Guwoo NFT: +5% SGR
  • Sporebot NFT: +1% (Common) to +5% (Super Rare)
  • LP NFTs: +2% to +10% (one-time promo)
Staking & MVP

SPORE Staking & MVP

Stake SPORES to earn passive rewards at 20% APR and accumulate Mycelium Voting Power (MVP) for governance. Use !stake to get started.

  • Every 100M SPORES staked = 1 Mycelium Node
  • Each node runs a 90-day cycle — every 90 days = +1 MVP per node
  • MVP rolls over infinitely (180 days = 2 MVP per node)
  • Fast Unstake (7 days): lose ALL MVP
  • Slow Unstake (42 days): keep ALL MVP

Daily Forage

How to Forage

Every day at 7:20 EST a fresh forage thread is pinned to r/MushroomPlanet. You have 24 hours to participate before it locks automatically.

  • Comment !forage once per day to earn SHROOMS and SPORES based on your streak
  • Come back daily — missing a day resets your streak to 0
  • Protect your streak with Vacation Days (earned every 7-day consecutive streak)
  • Any !forage has a 1-in-3000 chance to win the current Jackpot

Streak Reward Scale

  • Day 1: 1,000 SHROOMS | 100,000 SPORES
  • Scales continuously — max 6,874 SHROOMS and 687,400 SPORES at Day 420+

Jackpot Info

Comment Upvote Rewards

Monthly Comment Upvote Thread

Every month Sporebot posts a pinned Monthly Comment Upvote Thread. Any comments you leave anywhere on the subreddit that month earn you SHROOMS and SPORES based on their upvotes — paid out when the thread closes at the end of the month.

  • 200 SHROOMS + 20,000 SPORES per upvote on your comments
  • Only your top comment per post counts (no farming multiple comments on one post)
  • Bot commands, "good bot" phrases, and zero-upvote comments are excluded
  • Rewards are distributed automatically when the thread closes each month
  • Leaderboard updates live inside the pinned thread throughout the month
