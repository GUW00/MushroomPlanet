Harvesting

Once your farm reaches Sporulation (Day 42), use !harvest to advance your Farm Rank. Each harvest earns a permanent +3% yield bonus, a new custom name, and custom border colors in !farm.

🌲 Forage Streak Milestone Unlocks

As you build your forage streak, you unlock permanent items that boost your rewards:

  • Day 7: Golden Shovel — enables treasure finds in !dig
  • Day 21: Queen Totem — chance to earn bonus SPORES from !mist
  • Day 42: King Totem — chance to earn bonus SPORES from !fan
  • Day 69: Kid Totem — additional SPORE bonus
  • Day 100: Gold Totem — chance at an extra !toss

Vacation Days

Every 7 consecutive forage days you earn 1 Vacation Day. Vacation Days are spent automatically when you miss a forage — preserving your streak. Use !flute in Discord to check your Vacation Day balance and how the system works.

🧙 Visit System — Wimblecap

Use !visit to spend time with Wimblecap, the wise forest elder. Each visit counts toward your total visit milestone and can reward SHROOMS or special items.

  • 50th visit: Unlock the Elder Pipe — use !pipe to reset your daily cooldowns (14-day cooldown)
  • 100, 150, 200, 250, 300, 420 visits: Milestone SHROOM rewards
  • Every 30 visits after 450: Recurring SHROOM rewards

Wimblecap also lets you browse your collected Artifacts, Scrolls, and Items from the visit menu.

Reddit Guide

🌲 Daily Forage on Reddit

A new Daily Forage Thread is automatically posted every day on r/MushroomPlanet. Comment !forage once per day to earn rewards and grow your streak.

  • Rewards scale continuously with streak — max is 6,874 SHROOMS and 687,400 SPORES per day at Day 420+.
  • Missing a day resets your streak. Vacation Days (earned every 7 consecutive forage days) protect it automatically.
  • Use !sprout for a one-time new-user bonus: 1,000 SHROOMS + 100,000 SPORES.
  • Use !link 0xYourWallet to link your Polygon wallet and earn a bonus of 5,000 SHROOMS + 500,000 SPORES.

🤖 Sporebot vs. CCBot

Sporebot handles !forage, !faucet, and !link inside the r/MushroomPlanet Daily Thread and connects to Discord rewards.

CCBot (CommunityCurrency) handles wallet management and tips across Reddit broadly.

CCBot Commands

  • !register 0xYourWallet — Register your Polygon address
  • !tip 100 or !tip 100 SHROOM — Tip a user (minimum 10 SHROOM)
  • !withdraw 100 SHROOM — Withdraw to your wallet
  • !balance — Check your CCBot balance
