Sporebot — Two Ways to Play

🎮 Sporebot on Discord

The full Sporebot experience lives on Discord — farming, brewing, staking, digging, governance, and more. Verify first, then use !start in #farming to receive 1,000 SHROOMS and begin planting.

Getting Started

  • Verify in #verify (powered by Double Counter — checks for VPNs and alt accounts)
  • Use !start in #farming to claim your 1,000 SHROOMS
  • Use !plant 1000 to start your Mushroom Farm and begin earning SPORES passively
  • Register your Polygon wallet in #wallet-web3 with !register 0xYourAddress

Key Commands

  • Daily: !forage !mist !fan !explore !visit !dig !toss
  • Hourly: !gather !cave !feed
  • Farm: !farm !home !plant !harvest !freeze
  • Tokens: !withdraw !transfer !convert !stake
  • Brewing: !brew !potion !shop
  • Info: !sporebots /balance !nft

🌿 Sporebot on Reddit

A fresh Daily Forage Thread is posted automatically every day at 7:20 EST on r/MushroomPlanet and stays open for 24 hours. Build your streak daily for bigger rewards.

Key Reddit Commands

  • !forage — Earn SHROOMS and SPORES based on your streak (max 6,874 SHROOMS at Day 420+)
  • !sprout — One-time new-user bonus: 1,000 SHROOMS + 100,000 SPORES + 1 GS Token
  • !feed — Feed forest animals (costs 42-420 SHROOMS, 1-hour cooldown)
  • !tip [amount] [token] — Tip SHROOMS or SPORES to another user
  • !gs — Give a Goodshroom Token to a community member
  • !airdrop / !raffle — Distribute tokens or pick random winners
  • !balance — View your Reddit balances
  • !profile — View your Reddit stats and XP rank

🔗 Linking Reddit + Discord

Connect your Reddit and Discord accounts to unlock cross-platform rewards and transfer tokens between both ecosystems.

How to Link

  • In the Reddit Daily Thread, comment !register 0xYourWalletAddress to register your wallet
  • Then comment !link — you'll receive a DM from Pixie on Discord asking you to confirm
  • Once confirmed you receive a one-time bonus of 20,000 SHROOMS + 2,000,000 SPORES

After Linking

  • Use !transfer in Discord to move your Reddit SHROOMS and SPORES to your Discord wallet
  • Use !plant [amount] to plant transferred SHROOMS into your Discord farm
  • Reddit earning and Discord farming work independently — linking merges your balances on demand
  • You also earn 1 GS Token for every 5-day forage streak milestone (5, 10, 15...)

🌾 Farm System Overview

Your planted SHROOMS passively earn SPORES based on your Spore Generation Rate (SGR). The farm advances through 5 stages over 42 days. Harvest at Sporulation to rank up and increase your plant limit permanently.

Farm Ranks & Plant Limits

  • Bronze: 0% SGR | 2,000,000 plant limit
  • Silver: +3% SGR | 2,500,000 plant limit
  • Gold: +6% SGR | 3,000,000 plant limit
  • Emerald: +9% SGR | 3,500,000 plant limit
  • Diamond: +12% SGR | 4,200,000 plant limit
  • Master: +15% SGR | 4,500,000 plant limit
  • Grandmaster: +20% SGR | 5,000,000 plant limit
  • Legend: +25% SGR | 6,874,000 plant limit

Total SGR Sources (in-game cap: 100%)

  • Farm Rank: up to 25%
  • Farm Stage: up to 20% (Sporulation)
  • Potion Brew: up to 10%
  • 5 Gemstones: up to 25% (5% each)
  • In-Game Sporebot Bonus: up to 25% (community activity, 5 categories)
  • LP / Guwoo NFT / Sporebot NFT / LP NFTs: additional on-chain bonuses

After every harvest, remember to replant all your SHROOMS to resume earning.

🌀 SPORE Staking & MVP

Stake SPORES to earn passive rewards at 20% APR and accumulate Mycelium Voting Power (MVP) for governance. Use !stake to get started.

  • Every 100M SPORES staked = 1 Mycelium Node
  • Each node runs a 90-day cycle — every 90 days = +1 MVP per node
  • MVP rolls over infinitely (180 days = 2 MVP per node)
  • Fast Unstake (7 days): lose ALL MVP
  • Slow Unstake (42 days): keep ALL MVP

⛓️ Blockchain & Withdrawals

Connect your Polygon wallet in #wallet-web3 to unlock withdrawals and NFT verification.

  • !register 0xYourAddress — link your Polygon wallet
  • !nft — scan your wallet and verify NFT ownership
  • !withdraw [amount] shrooms or !withdraw [amount] spores
  • Daily limits: 1,000,000 SHROOMS and 100,000,000 SPORES

🧪 Free Trial & Requirements

New users get a 42-day free trial. After that, meet ONE of the following to keep farming:

  • Option 1: Hold 500,000+ SHROOM-equivalent on-chain for 3+ consecutive farming days
  • Option 2: Hold 2 Sporebots OR any 1 Mooshie NFT (House, King, Queen, Gold, Kid Shroom) — 3+ days
  • Option 3: Provide liquidity (LP Bonus of 0.1 or higher) — qualifies immediately

Reddit Guide

🌿 Daily Forage

Every day at 7:20 EST a fresh forage thread is pinned to the top of r/MushroomPlanet. You have 24 hours to participate before it locks automatically.

  • Comment !forage once per day to earn SHROOMS and SPORES based on your streak
  • Come back daily — missing a day resets your streak to 0
  • Protect your streak with Vacation Days (earned every 7-day consecutive streak)
  • Any !forage has a 1-in-3000 chance to win the current Jackpot

Streak Reward Scale

  • Day 1: 1,000 SHROOMS | 100,000 SPORES
  • Scales continuously with streak — max 6,874 SHROOMS and 687,400 SPORES at Day 420+

🌱 Sprout & GS Tokens

  • New Shroomies can comment !sprout for a one-time gift: 1,000 SHROOMS + 100,000 SPORES + 1 GS Token
  • Earn 1 GS Token for every 5-day forage streak milestone (5, 10, 15, 20...)
  • Use !gs on any post or comment to gift a GS Token to someone — each delivers 6,874 SPORES

🐾 Feed the Animals

  • Comment !feed to feed the forest creatures — costs 42-420 SHROOMS per feed
  • Has a 1-hour cooldown
  • Every 6,874 SHROOMS fed increases your Forage Bonus
  • The community Jackpot grows with every !feed until a winner is picked via !forage

💸 Tipping, Airdrops & Raffles

  • !tip [amount] [token] — tip on a post or comment
  • !tip [amount] [token] u/username — tip someone directly
  • !gs — give a Goodshroom Token (reply to a post or comment)
  • !airdrop [shrooms/spores] — send equal tips to all commenters on your post
  • !raffle [# of winners] — pick random winner(s) from your post's comments

Tipping & Raffle Post Policy

  • Only 1 tipping OR raffle post per user per week, unless total tips exceed 50,000 SHROOMS
  • Posts must use flair: "Don't Mind Tipping" or "Raffle"
  • Minimum tip per comment: 420 SHROOMS from OP
  • Post must total at least 5,000 SHROOMS in tips from OP to others
  • NFT raffles and auctions are exempt from the weekly limit
  • Non-compliant posts will be demonetized
