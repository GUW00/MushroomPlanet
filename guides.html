📅 Daily Commands

Daily commands reset once per day and are used in #daily-forage. Hourly commands can be used multiple times and go in #hourly-tasks.

Daily (once per day)

  • !forage — Earn SHROOMS based on your streak (up to 6,874/day at Day 420+)
  • !mist & !fan — Earn random SHROOMS and maintain your farm
  • !explore — Discover scrolls, artifacts, secret gardens, and recipes
  • !visit — Visit Wimblecap daily to earn rewards and learn about your items
  • !dig — Find SHROOMS, SPORES, crypto, NFTs, or a Golden Chest
  • !toss — Earn various rewards including the rare Sporulation Jackpot

Hourly (multiple times per day)

  • !gather — Collect herbs to use in brewing potions
  • !cave — Navigate the cave puzzle for gemstones
  • !feed — Feed forest animals (requires Wildtongue Gemstone)

Other Useful Commands

  • !plant [amount] — Plant SHROOMS into your farm
  • !farm — View your farm details and SGR
  • !home — View cooldowns, items, stats, and more
  • !sporebots — View your in-game Sporebot and NFT Sporebot helper
  • !convert [amount] — Convert at 100:1 ratio (100 SPORES = 1 SHROOM)
  • !transfer [amt] [token] — Transfer from Reddit to your Discord wallet
  • !shop — Sell gemstones, buy herbs, craft ingredients
  • !freeze — Freeze your farm to protect your forage streak (SGR stops)
  • !brew — Overview of brewing and detailed instructions
  • !stake — View your stake or get started staking SPORES
  • /balance — View Farm, Discord, Reddit and on-chain balances plus NFTs

🌀 SPORE Staking & MVP

Stake SPORES to earn passive rewards and build Mycelium Voting Power (MVP) for governance. Use !stake to get started.

Staking Rate

  • 20% APR flat rate for all stakers
  • Every 100M SPORES staked = 1 Mycelium Node
  • Each node runs a 90-day cycle — every 90 days = +1 MVP per node
  • MVP accumulates infinitely (180 days = 2 MVP per node)

Unstaking Options

  • Fast Unstake (7 days): Lose ALL MVP, get SPORES back faster
  • Slow Unstake (42 days): Keep ALL MVP, longer wait

SGR stops immediately when you unstake (either option).

What MVP Is Used For

  • Governance voting weight on proposals
  • Influencing Sporebot decisions
  • Higher MVP = more influence

⏳ Free Trial & Minimum Requirements

New users get a 42-day free trial to explore Sporebot without needing a wallet or NFTs. Once the trial ends, meet ONE of the following to keep farming:

  • Option 1 — On-chain holdings: Hold 500,000+ SHROOM-equivalent on-chain for at least 3 consecutive farming days (1 SHROOM = 100 SPORES for equivalency)
  • Option 2 — NFT holdings: Hold 2 Sporebots OR any 1 Mooshie NFT (House, King, Queen, Gold, Kid Shroom) — must be held for 3+ days
  • Option 3 — Liquidity Provider: Earn an LP Bonus of 0.1 or higher by providing liquidity to SHROOM/WETH or SPORE/WETH V2 pools — LP holders qualify immediately

🤖 Sporebot vs. CCBot

Sporebot handles !forage, !faucet, and !link inside the r/MushroomPlanet Daily Thread and connects to Discord rewards.

CCBot (CommunityCurrency) handles wallet management and tips across Reddit broadly.

CCBot Commands
