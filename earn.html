🌀 SPORE Staking and Governance

Stake your SPORES with !stake in Discord to earn 20% APR passively and accumulate Mycelium Voting Power (MVP) for ecosystem governance proposals.

  • Every 100M SPORES staked = 1 Mycelium Node
  • Each node runs a 90-day cycle - every 90 days = +1 MVP per node
  • MVP rolls over infinitely (180 days = 2 MVP per node, and so on)
  • Fast Unstake (7 days): lose ALL MVP accumulated
  • Slow Unstake (42 days): keep ALL MVP accumulated

  • !tip [amount] [token] - tip any post or comment
